Instructions:
	- Read the instructions on the bottom left of the game screen
	- Move the trash into the correct bin according to the on-screen instructions
	- Fight against time and put the trash in the correct bins to increase your reputation
	- Bring the reputation bar to the top to get a promotion
	- If your reputation falls to zero... your supervisor might just have a word with you...
